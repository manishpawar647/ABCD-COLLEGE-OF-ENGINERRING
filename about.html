About ABCD

ABCD College of Engineering (An International Institute), is one among the premier technical institutes in world state in the un-aided sector. Established in 1983 by the ABCD Educational Society under the charismatic leadership of Shri. Manish Patil, the Institute had its vision for social transformation and , upliftment of rural masses through education, training and research.
